Approved copy · 80 words

Lightio is a free iOS app that combines on-device object detection with AI-powered Hadith search. Built by a two-person team in Yogyakarta, Indonesia, Lightio lets users discover authentic Hadiths simply by pointing their iPhone camera at everyday objects — a date, a cup, a tree — while keeping all image processing on-device. The app supports prayer reminders with 8 global calculation methods and gives users granular control over data and privacy.
