## About Lightio

Lightio is an iOS app that combines on-device object detection with AI-powered Hadith search. Point your camera at everyday objects — a date, a cup, a tree — and Lightio surfaces authentic Hadiths connected to what you see. Available worldwide, with prayer reminders supporting 8 global calculation methods.

## Account & Data Management

You can manage your account, consent settings, and personal data directly inside the app:

* **Sign in:** Use Sign in with Apple, email & password, or verify your phone number.
* **Consent:** Toggle Analytics, UI Personalization, Data Personalization, Cloud Sync, and Prayer Reminders independently in Privacy & Data settings.
* **Export or delete your data:** Use the Data Dashboard in Settings to export or permanently delete your history, favorites, and profile.

## Common Issues

Quick fixes for the most frequent issues:

1. **Detection isn't working** — check camera permission in iOS Settings, ensure good lighting, try a different angle.
2. **Prayer notifications missing** — confirm Prayer Reminders is on, check iOS notification settings, verify location is set.
3. **Sync isn't working** — ensure Cloud Storage is enabled on both devices, signed in with the same account, iCloud has free storage.
4. **Wrong prayer times** — different communities use different methods; match your local authority (MWL, ISNA, Egypt, Umm al-Qura, Karachi, Muhammadiyah, Kemenag, or Tehran).
